# Black screen after idle time / hibernation / suspend

## What happens
Sometimes screen is completely black even though computer is on. The computer may have been running idle for a while before it happens.

## Why it happens

(Unknown)

## Solution ##

Switch to another TTY (terminal) by using a key combination: Ctrl + Alt + F1 ... F6.

Wait for a couple of seconds and then return to the desktop environment by pressing: Ctrl + Alt + F2 or F7. Usually the desktop environment is on F2 or F7.

It may take a couple of seconds to load the screen.
